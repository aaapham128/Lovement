Sample Article:

The Affordable Care Act's policy that protected transgender patients from being discriminated against health care insurance providers on the Affordable Care Act was reversed by the Trump Administration. It means that transgender people who want to transition by using hormonal pills and sex reassignment surgery have a harder time having their prescriptions filled and paid for. The cause of the struggle is that health care providers and medical professionals refuse to serve those who identify as LGBTQ+. This leads to the question of what can these transgender teens, adults, and youth do if they are refused health care coverage. Unfortunately, most middle- to low-income families do not have enough resources to cover the expensive surgery without insurance paying for some of the cost.
